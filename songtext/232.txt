2.    Praise Him! Praise Him—
    Jesus our blessed Redeemer!
    For our sins, He suffered
        and bled and died.
    He, our Rock, our hope
        of eternal salvation.
    Hail Him! Hail Him—
    Jesus the Crucified.
    Sound His praises!
    Jesus Who bore our sorrows,
    Love unbounded, wonderful,
        deep and strong.
